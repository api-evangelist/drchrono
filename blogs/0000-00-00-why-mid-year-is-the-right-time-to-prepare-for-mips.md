The post argues for mid-year MIPS preparation rather than a Q4 rush, noting that summer reviews provide time to address documentation gaps, retrain staff, and improve metrics before deadlines. It recommends a five-step action plan and using integrated EHR systems to streamline data collection and performance tracking throughout the year.
